Bill Gates is an American businessman, investor, philanthropist,
and writer. He is best known for co-founding the software company
Microsoft with his childhood friend Paul Allen. During his career at
Microsoft, Gates held various positions, including chairman, CEO,
president, and chief software architect. He played a pivotal role in
the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s. Born and
raised in Seattle, Washington, Gates dropped out of Harvard to
start Microsoft. Beyond his business endeavors, he is also a
prominent philanthropist, focusing on global health, education,
and climate change through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
His impact on technology and society is lasting, making computers an
indispensable part of everyday life
